\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\mbox{\hyperlink{classoomph_1_1TreeRoot}{Tree\+Root}} is a \mbox{\hyperlink{classoomph_1_1Tree}{Tree}} that forms the root of a (recursive) tree. The \char`\"{}root node\char`\"{} is special as it holds additional information about its neighbours and their relative rotation (inside a \mbox{\hyperlink{classoomph_1_1TreeForest}{Tree\+Forest}}).
