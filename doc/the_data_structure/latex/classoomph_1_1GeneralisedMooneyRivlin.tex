\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////// ///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////// 

Generalisation of Mooney Rivlin constitutive law to compressible media as suggested on p. 553 of Fung, Y.\+C. \& Tong, P. \char`\"{}\+Classical and \+Computational Solid Mechanics\char`\"{} World Scientific (2001). Input parameters are Young\textquotesingle{}s modulus E, Poisson ratio nu and the Mooney-\/\+Rivlin constant C1. In the small-\/deformation-\/limit the behaviour becomes equivalent to that of linear elasticity with the same E and nu.

Note that there\textquotesingle{}s a factor of 2 difference between C1 and the Mooney Rivlin C1!
