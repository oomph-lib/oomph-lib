\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\mbox{\hyperlink{classoomph_1_1OcTreeRoot}{Oc\+Tree\+Root}} is a \mbox{\hyperlink{classoomph_1_1OcTree}{Oc\+Tree}} that forms the root of a (recursive) octree. The \char`\"{}root node\char`\"{} is special as it holds additional information about its neighbours and their relative rotation (inside a \mbox{\hyperlink{classoomph_1_1OcTreeForest}{Oc\+Tree\+Forest}}).
