8  We consider the uniform steady thermal expansion of an elastic
9  body that is differentially heated. The top surface is heated and the bottom
10  surface is maintained at the reference temperature, which leads to
11  a uniform temperature gradient throughout the material. The material
12  expands more near the upper surface than near the lower surface, deforming
13  the initially rectangular block into an curved configuration.
